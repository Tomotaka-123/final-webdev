Course Title

Class: Cybersecurity

Semester: Spring 2023

Description: This course provides an introduction to cybersecurity and prepares students for the CompTIA Security+ certification exam. The first six weeks of the course will cover topics such as the fundamentals of cybersecurity, threats and vulnerabilities, network security, cryptography, and security principles. Students will also gain practical experience through labs and packet tracer exercises. The remaining weeks will focus on a review of the CompTIA Security+ certification exam objectives. Students will cover topics such as security risk management, access control, identity and access management, and secure network architecture. The course will also cover security assessments and incident response. In the final weeks, students will complete a project that demonstrates their understanding of the course material, and take the CompTIA Security+ certification exam. By the end of this course, students will have a solid foundation in cybersecurity and be prepared to take the CompTIA Security+ certification exam, a valuable industry certification in the field of cybersecurity.

Topics Covered:

  • What Cybersecurity is
  • Demonstrating Cyber Attacks
  • Discussing Worm Book
  • ...

Skills Learned:
